About Us

At Trustopay, we’re on a mission to revolutionize how freelancers, agencies, and clients collaborate in today’s digital economy. Founded with the vision of building trust in every transaction, we offer a secure, transparent, and seamless platform that ensures payment security, clear contracts, and fair project management for both parties.

We know the challenges that freelancers and agencies face: delayed payments, unfair scope changes, and clients not honoring agreements. On the flip side, clients often worry about the quality of service, hidden fees, and not receiving what they’ve paid for. Trustopay eliminates these issues by acting as a trusted escrow service, holding payments securely until the agreed-upon milestones are met, and ensuring both sides are protected.

Our Mission

To build a platform where trust is the foundation of every project, ensuring that freelancers, agencies, and clients can work together with confidence and security.

  • Transparency

    Clear, detailed contracts and open communication throughout the project lifecycle.

  • Security

    Payments are securely held in escrow, giving both freelancers and clients peace of mind.

  • Fairness

    A balanced platform that provides fair payment for work delivered and protects clients from unmet expectations.

  • Innovation

    Leveraging AI-powered tools to create contracts in minutes, enabling faster and more efficient project management.

Our Solution

Trustopay simplifies the entire freelancing and agency-client interaction:

  • AI-Powered Contract Creation
  • Secure Payments via Escrow
  • Dispute Resolution
  • Milestone-Based Payments

Why Trustopay?

With the rise of digital freelancing and remote work, trust is more important than ever. We created Trustopay to eliminate the uncertainties that often arise in freelance and client relationships. By providing a platform that handles contracts, payments, and project management, we ensure that both freelancers and clients can focus on what they do best—delivering and receiving high-quality work without worrying about payments or project scope.

Built in India, Trusted Worldwide

We’re proud to say that Trustopay was built in Gujarat, India, and is helping freelancers and agencies across the globe handle their projects with confidence and security.
